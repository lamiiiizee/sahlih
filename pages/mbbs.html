STUDY MBBS IN KYRGYSTAN

Kyrgyzstan offers an excellent opportunity for international students to pursue MBBS studies with its affordable tuition fees, globally recognized universities, and a diverse cultural experience.

QUICK FACTS

  • The capital city of Kyrgyzstan is Bishkek.
  • As of 2021, the estimated population of Kyrgyzstan was around 6.5 million people.
  • The official language is Kyrgyz, and Russian is also widely spoken.
  • The official currency of Kyrgyzstan is the Kyrgyzstani Som (KGS).

STUDY MBBS IN UZBEKISTHAN

Uzbekistan is a landlocked country in Central Asia known for its rich history, diverse cultural heritage, and beautiful architecture, and it offers opportunities for international students to pursue MBBS studies in reputed universities.

QUICK FACTS

  • The capital city of Uzbekistan is Tashkent, which is also the largest city in the country.
  • The official language of Uzbekistan is Uzbek, which is a Turkic language.
  • Uzbekistan gained its independence from the Soviet Union in 1991 when the USSR dissolved.

STUDY MBBS IN ARMANIA

Armenia, located in the South Caucasus region, is a country known for its rich history, culture, and picturesque landscapes. It has become a popular destination for international students, including those pursuing MBBS studies, due to its reputable medical universities and affordable tuition fees.

QUICK FACTS

  • The capital and largest city of Armenia is Yerevan, which is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.
  • As of my last update, the estimated population of Armenia was around 2.9 million people.
  • Armenian culture is known for its unique traditions, dance, and cuisine, including dishes like dolma and lavash.

STUDY MBBS IN GEORGIA

Georgia is a country located at the crossroads of Eastern Europe and Western Asia. It offers MBBS courses with high-quality medical education and affordable tuition fees, attracting many international students to study medicine in Georgia.

QUICK FACTS

  • Tbilisi is the capital and largest city of Georgia.
  • The official language is Georgian.
  • The currency is the Georgian lari (GEL).
  • Georgia is one of the oldest wine-producing regions globally, with a tradition of winemaking that dates back thousands of years.

STUDY MBBS IN RUSSIA

Russia is the largest country in the world, spanning Eastern Europe and Northern Asia. It offers MBBS courses in top medical universities with modern facilities and a diverse clinical exposure, attracting a significant number of international students to pursue medical education in Russia.

QUICK FACTS

  • Russia is the largest country in the world by land area, spanning across Eastern Europe and Northern Asia.
  • Moscow is the capital and the largest city in Russia.
  • The official language is Russian.
  • Russia has a rich cultural heritage, famous for its literature, music, dance, and arts.

The Philippines is an archipelago in Southeast Asia known for its diverse culture, stunning landscapes, warm hospitality. It offers medical courses, including MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery), providing international students with an opportunity to pursue quality medical education in a tropical setting.

  • The country is known for its rich biodiversity, hosting a wide variety of plant and animal species.
